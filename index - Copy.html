Is it for me?

What kinds of issues can this work help?

  • Feeling tired
  • Overwhelm
  • Lack of direction
  • Loss of creativity
  • Anxiety
  • Difficulty with relationships
  • Muddled or clouded thinking
  • Loss of will

People come to this work seeking a path to clarity with a desire to get energized, and reconnect to their creativity. In the process, you will likely find an increase in empathy along with energy and body wisdom that supports you to trust, enjoy and express yourself more fully.

What if I’m already working with another type of healing?

It’s not unusual for people to begin this integrative work on the referral of another practitioner. Often, this layer of support collaborates with other types of healing such as acupuncture, bodywork, or what you might think of as ‘traditional’ talk therapy. I’m happy to connect with you or your current practitioners to explore the synergies within the work. Some indicators that you might benefit from additional support are:

  • You experience emotional release in sessions but are not able to integrate the work and experience life differently.
  • Your sessions tend to be overrun by your ongoing day to day crises, and you feel unable to find a place of stability from which to delve into underlying issues.
  • You’re in your head trying to make sense of an experience while struggling to feel it in your body. This may be a sign that addressing the ‘body armor’ first will allow access to and healing of a past trauma.
  • You find yourself chasing emotional release/discharge versus sustainable experience of energy within the body to build an ongoing sense of well-being.
  • You’ve found yourself jumping to different offerings, trying to escape emotional dilemmas or ease pain.
  • Your boundaries between self and others are minimal or non-existent.

Do you see clients out of area?

Yes. I have been providing virtual sessions for over ten years and have found this work translates effectively. I have an active international client base.
